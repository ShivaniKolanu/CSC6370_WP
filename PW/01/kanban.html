Kanban Methodology

How we implemented Kanban Methodology?

  • Started by defining the workflow process for our project. Mapped out the steps involved in completing the work, including who is responsible for each step and the expected timeline.
  • Used sticky notes to represent the tasks that needed to be completed.
  • We have set a time for meeting to meet daily in order to discuss the blockers and the updates on the project.

How did we benefit?

  • We were able to track the progress through sticky notes and identify the areas which needed attention.
  • We were able to collaborate and communicate better which helped us in problem-solving and decision-making.
  • By prioritizing the tasks, we were able to increase productivity and accuracy in the project.
  • This methodology helped us to continuously improve the process and optimize workflow.
