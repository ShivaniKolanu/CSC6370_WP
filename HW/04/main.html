Below are some of the instructions to play this game.

1. Select the game type from above as this work has 3 types of games which is 8 images, 10 images and 12 images.
2. Clicking on one of it starts your game.
3. 8 images game is going to give you 120 seconds to complete the puzzle.
4. 10 images game is going to give you 150 seconds to complete the puzzle.
5. 12 images game is going to give you 180 seconds to complete the puzzle.
6. Your goal is to match each of the pictures with its copy, Click the two matching images to reveal them. Please wait for images to disappear, and then make the next attempt.
7. Reload the page if time is up and restart the game again.
8. Keep playing and enjoy!
